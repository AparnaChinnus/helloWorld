Fourth Industrial Revolution

Wednesday 28th Feb 2018

Patent applications for smart object technologies, on which the Fourth Industrial Revolution depends, are rising rapidly, according to a new study. More than 5,000 “Internet of Things” (IoT) patent applications were filed in 2016 at the European Patent Office (EPO). This marks a growth rate of 54% over the last three years. Compared to the 8% growth rate for all patent applications, IoT technologies are rapidly attracting inventors.
